Research Resources

The Ji Laboratory is committed to open science and the sharing of research resources with the scientific community. On this page, you'll find a collection of protocols, datasets, software tools, and educational materials developed by our lab or that we find useful in our research.

These resources are designed to support research in pulmonary biology, particularly focused on ARDS, COVID-19, and sarcoidosis. If you have questions about any of these resources or would like additional information, please contact us.
