Research & Publications

Our scholarly contributions to the field of pulmonary research

Research Focus

The Ji Laboratory studies the molecular mechanisms underlying acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS), COVID-19, and sarcoidosis. Our research aims to identify new endotypes, regenerative signaling pathways, biomarkers in extracellular vesicles, and host-pathogen interactions in pulmonary diseases.

Our key research areas include:

  • Epithelial Sodium Channels in Lung Injury - Investigating the role of ENaC in the pathogenesis of lung edema using humanized SCNN1D knockin mouse models
  • COVID-19 and ARDS Pathogenesis - Studying the critical role of fibrinolysis and lung epithelial proteins in COVID-19 and edema fluid resolution
  • Surfactant Protein Dysfunction - Examining how SFTPC and SFTPB gene mutations affect alveolar epithelial cell fate in fibrotic lungs
  • Multi-Omics Approaches - Applying cutting-edge techniques including scRNAseq, metaproteomics, and machine learning to identify biomarkers
